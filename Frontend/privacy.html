Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Kamio Games is committed to protecting the privacy of our players. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage information when you play Memories We Never Had on Android and PC.

1. Data Collection

Our games are designed for offline play. We do not actively collect, store, or share sensitive personal data such as your name, email address, or specific location.

2. Device Access Rights

On the Android platform, games may require storage access permission solely to store your game progress data (save files) locally on your own device.

3. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time as game features evolve. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us through our official support channels.
