Our Story

Kamio Games was founded with a simple vision: to create story-driven games that leave a lasting impression on players. What began as an independent passion project has grown into a small game development studio dedicated to crafting meaningful visual novel experiences.

Every project is developed with a strong focus on storytelling, memorable characters, and immersive presentation. Rather than pursuing quantity, Kamio Games aims to create games that players can genuinely connect with and remember long after the credits roll.

Our first title, Our Spring Together, marks the beginning of this journey. As the studio continues to grow, future projects will explore new stories, new characters, and new worlds while maintaining the same commitment to quality and heartfelt storytelling.

This is only the beginning of Kamio Games, and we hope you'll continue this journey with us.

Studio Profile

About Kamio Games

Kamio Games is an independent game development studio based in Indonesia, dedicated to creating story-driven experiences that emphasize emotional storytelling, memorable characters, and immersive presentation.

Founded with a passion for interactive narratives, the studio focuses primarily on visual novels and narrative-based games. Every project is carefully crafted to deliver meaningful stories while maintaining high standards in visual design, user experience, and overall production quality.

The studio's debut title, Our Spring Together, marks the beginning of Kamio Games' journey in bringing original stories to players on Android through Google Play. Looking ahead, Kamio Games is committed to expanding its portfolio with new projects that explore diverse themes, unique characters, and richer gameplay experiences.

As an independent studio, Kamio Games believes that every game is more than entertainment—it is a story waiting to be experienced and remembered.
